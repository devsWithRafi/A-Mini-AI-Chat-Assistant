- Euler formula: e to the power (ix) = cos(x) + i times sin(x)

- Regression analysis, correlation coefficient

- Permutations: nPr = n! / (n-r)!

#### Applied Mathematics

Physics Formulas:
- Newton second law: Force = mass times acceleration (F = ma)
- Einstein mass-energy: E = mc squared
- Kinetic energy: KE = (1/2) times mass times velocity squared
- Potential energy: PE = mass times gravity times height
- Work: W = Force times distance times cos(theta)
- Power: P = Work / time
- Momentum: p = mass times velocity
- Impulse: J = Force times time = change in momentum
- Centripetal force: F = (mass times velocity squared) / radius
- Universal gravitation: F = G times (m1 times m2) / r squared
- Coulomb law: F = k times (q1 times q2) / r squared
- Ohm law: V = I times R (Voltage = Current times Resistance)
- Wave speed: v = frequency times wavelength
- Doppler effect formulas
- Projectile motion equations
- Simple harmonic motion: x = A times cos(omega times t)

Chemistry Formulas:
- Ideal gas law: PV = nRT
- pH = -log[H+]
- Molarity: M = moles / liters
- Percent yield = (actual yield / theoretical yield) times 100
- Rate laws and reaction kinetics
- Equilibrium constant expressions
- Thermodynamics: Gibbs free energy, enthalpy, entropy

Finance and Economics:
- Compound interest: A = P times (1 + r/n) to the power (nt)

- Net present value (NPV)
- Internal rate of return (IRR)
- Black-Scholes option pricing
- Elasticity of demand
- Break-even analysis

Engineering:
